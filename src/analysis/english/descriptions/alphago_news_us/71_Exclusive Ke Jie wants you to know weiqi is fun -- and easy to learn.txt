Ke Jie, the world's best weiqi (Go) player, cried after losing 0-3 to the artificial intelligence program AlphaGo. He took off his glasses and wiped his eyes. But he's happy to see the world focus on his beloved sport. The star player did not talk much about future. He needed some rest, after the high-profile matches. But Ke's passion for weiqi has not wavered.
