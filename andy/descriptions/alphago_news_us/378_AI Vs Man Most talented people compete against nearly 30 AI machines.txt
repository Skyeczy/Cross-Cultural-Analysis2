"Ji Zhi Guo Ren" (AI Vs Man) is a TV show about to air in China. The human team will compete against nearly 30 AI machines. They include a poetry-writing robot and a robot that can shoot hoops. The team is made up of top talents including world champions. By working with the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the program found the leading AI projects across China.
