더 강해진 알파고, '세계 1위' 커제에 첫판 완승The world’s top-ranked Go player, Ke Jie, has lost the first of three matches against his AI adversary Alpha Go.Developed by Google, Alpha Go managed to beat Ke Jie by only half a point on Tuesday. The match was held in the Chinese city of Wuzhen and the two will face off again on Thursday and Saturday. The world number one stands to win one-and-a-half million U.S. dollars if he beats his AI rival.He had initially refused to play the machine after it beat 18-time world champion Lee Sedol of Korea last March.
